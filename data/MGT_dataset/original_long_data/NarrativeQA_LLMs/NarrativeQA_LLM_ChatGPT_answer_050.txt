The purpose of the fiddlefest concert in the context is to raise money so that the string program, which is at risk of being cut due to budgetary constraints, can continue. Roberta guaspari, the protagonist, enlists the support of former pupils, parents, and teachers to organize the concert as a means of fundraising. With the help of other famous musicians, the concert is mounted at carnegie hall and becomes a resounding success, enabling the continuation of the program.
