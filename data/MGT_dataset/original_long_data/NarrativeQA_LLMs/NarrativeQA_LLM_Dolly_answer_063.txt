Domino, a former model living in los angeles, becomes a bounty hunter after she is kicked out of college. Claremont williams iii, a bail bondsman who also runs an armored car business, sets up the robbery of $10 million from drake bishop, the owner of the stratosphere hotel and casino in las vegas. Lateesha rodriguez, a criminal psychologist, has an affair with claremont and her counterfeit driver's license racket at the dmv gives the fbi information that leads to the fbi suspecting lateesha in a counterfeit driver's license ring, which will lead to lateesha being jailed. Lateesha tries to abort the stratosphere hotel heist but claremont, knowing the criminals involved, informs the criminals about lateesha and her counterfeit driver's license ring. They manage to steal the $10 million but claremont was their getaway driver. Claremont turns up at the stratosphere hotel with lateesha and an armed crew, and when the police arrive shoot the getaway driver, and domino is the only survivor.
