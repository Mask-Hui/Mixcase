Paul darnley has been avoiding going home because he has been avoiding his elderly wife, mrs. Darnley, for the past eighteen months. Mrs. Darnley has an irrational determination to convince her atheist and materialist husband of the existence of hell, and she has been traveling abroad with virginia st. John, the novel's hero. Paul has been avoiding mrs. Darnley because he doesn't want to be influenced by her beliefs and convince her of the existence of hell.
