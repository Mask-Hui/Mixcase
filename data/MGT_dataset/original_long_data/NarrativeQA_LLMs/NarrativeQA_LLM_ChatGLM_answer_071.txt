Diana best describes as a young woman who has been raised in an unconventional and difficult way due to her parents' death and her father's suicide. She is tomboyish and independent, with a freedom that is only allowed to boys. Before her journey, she refuses a proposal of marriage and doesn't know what love is. She is kidnapped and rapes by a sheik, but regains his trust over time. She is eventually kidnapped again by a rival sheik and takes a job as a riding instructor to try and regain her independence. She is passionate about riding and is in love with a man who misunderstands her love. In the end, she is saved by the man she loves and they declare their love for each other.
