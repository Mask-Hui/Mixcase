Being a child of a religious parent doesn't mean you have to be complacent and not be curious of the world around you. You have to ask questions and embrace challenges. One of his most strict rules is that he doesn't have a girlfriend until he is married. This might not seem very important to you now but it is quite a struggle for him, at 18 years old, to get a girlfriend, especially in a small town like this. He has to fight against the odds, the popular kids in school, all the girls and the alcohol. The point is that he should not be dependent on the almighty relationships or the power dynamics of the world. He needs to be prepared to take on challenges and rise to them. Because that is what a champion would do.
