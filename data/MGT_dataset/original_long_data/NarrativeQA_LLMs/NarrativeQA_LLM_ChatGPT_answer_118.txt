What is the movie and what is it about? the movie is "the damned united" and it is about the career of brian clough, a former football player who became a manager, focusing on his brief and controversial tenure as manager of leeds united in 1974, during which he alienated the team and fans. The movie also delves into clough's personality and his rivalry with leeds, in particular with their former manager don revie. It is based on the novel of the same name by david peace. The film stars michael sheen as brian clough, timothy spall as peter taylor, colm meaney as don revie, and jim broadbent as sam longson.
