Leonard becomes unhappy with the restrictions placed upon him as a patient of the hospital and desires the freedom to come and go as he pleases. He stirs up a revolt by arguing his case to dr. Sayer and the hospital administration. However, as the film progresses, leonard's physical condition deteriorates, and he eventually returns to his catatonic state.
