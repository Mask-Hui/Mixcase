Paul believes that he is 108 years old and does not yet die from his bladder infection. John, the black man whom paul was assigned to die alongside, is a rapist and murderer who is put to death. He murder melinda moores, the wife of the prison chief warden, and consequently becomes plagued by an unidentified brain tumor. John sacrifices himself to kill the man whom he had earlier raped and murdered, who had been sent to be his executioner.
