What is the plot of the movie "jason x"? in "jason x," which is the tenth installment of the "friday the 13th" series, jason voorhees, a notorious mass murderer, is cryogenically frozen and preserved for over four centuries. In the year 2455, a group of students discovers jason's frozen body and brings him aboard their spaceship, where he is accidentally reanimated. The students and crew members attempt to escape from jason, who goes on a killing spree aboard the spaceship. After many attempts, they seemingly manage to kill him, but he is rebuilt into a more powerful cyborg. Finally, in a dramatic confrontation, a survivor of the attack sacrifices himself to send jason to his final demise, but not before his charred mask sinks to the bottom of a lake on earth two.
