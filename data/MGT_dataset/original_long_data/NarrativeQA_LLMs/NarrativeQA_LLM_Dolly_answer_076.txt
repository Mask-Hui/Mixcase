The main conflict of the story concerns a love triangle. Paula, the main character of the story, is a vivacious, athletic, sexually self-aware woman who cannot bear children, who is subject to insomnia, and who is in love with two different men. One of these men is dick forrest, who is also a long-time lover of paula. The other man is charmian, a poetess who resembles paula physically, although paula does not recognize her.
