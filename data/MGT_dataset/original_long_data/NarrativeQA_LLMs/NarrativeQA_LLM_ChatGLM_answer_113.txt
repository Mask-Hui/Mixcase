Why is grassou still unhappy, even after marriage? grassou remains bitter that he is not a true artist. He is unable to eradicate from his heart a fatal thought, namely, that artists laugh at his work; that his name is a term of contempt in the studios; and that the feuilletons take no notice of his pictures. Despite his advantageous marriage, grassou remains bitter and unhappy because he feels that he is not a true artist and does not possess the talent and recognition that he deserves.
