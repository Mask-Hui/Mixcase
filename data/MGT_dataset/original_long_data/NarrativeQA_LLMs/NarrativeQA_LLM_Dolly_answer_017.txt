Zurich, switzerland, is a fictional location featured in the tv series "the sopranos". According to the show, zurich is a large city in switzerland that has become a world-wide hub for the human and crime organizations that control organized crime in new jersey. One of the main characters in the show, bobby cutler, is offered a position as a soprano in the fictional jamesinterval security, a company based out of zurich, switzerland. The company's headquarters are in a building which shares the same floor with the offices for tgi fridays.
