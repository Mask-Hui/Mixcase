The passage is describing the political system of the yuan dynasty, which was created by kublai khan. The system was based on a compromise between mongolian feudalism and traditional chinese autocratic-bureaucratic rule. However, the educated chinese elite were not given the same degree of esteem as they had been under the native chinese dynasties. The mongols and semuren were given certain advantages in the dynasty, and this would last even after the restoration of the imperial examination in the early 14th century. The unequal treatment may have been due to the fear of transferring power to the ethnic chinese under their rule
