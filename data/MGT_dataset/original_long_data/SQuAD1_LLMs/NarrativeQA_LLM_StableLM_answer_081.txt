The united methodist church is the largest denomination within the wider methodist movement, which has approximately 80 million adherents across the world. In the united states, the umc ranks as the largest mainline protestant denomination, the largest protestant church after the southern baptist convention, and the third largest christian denomination. As of 2014, worldwide membership was about 12 million: 7.2 million in the united states, and 4.4 million in africa, asia and europe.
