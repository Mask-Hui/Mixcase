Major events also play a big part in tourism in victoria, particularly cultural tourism and sports tourism. Most of these events are centred on melbourne, but others occur in regional cities, such as the v8 supercars and australian motorcycle grand prix at phillip island, the grand annual steeplechase at warrnambool and the australian international airshow at geelong. Festivals include the popular port fairy folk festival, queenscliff music festival, bells beach surfclassic and the bright autumn festival.
