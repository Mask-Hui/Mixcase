The summers in fresno are hot and dry with considerable sunshine, with july peaking at 97% of the total possible sunlight hours. Normally, there are 32 days of 100 °f (37.8 °c)+ highs and 106 days of 90 °f (32.2 °c)+ highs, and in july and august, there are only three or four days where the high does not reach 90 °f (32.2 °c).
