The sinclair broadcast group is the largest operator of abc stations by numerical total, owning or providing services to 28 abc affiliates and two additional subchannel-only affiliates; sinclair owns the largest abc subchannel affiliate by market size, wabm-dt2/wdbb-dt2 in the birmingham market, which serve as repeaters of wbma-ld (the largest low-power "big four" affiliate by market size, which itself is also simulcast on a subchannel of former wbma satellite wgww, owned by sinclair partner company howard stirk holdings).
