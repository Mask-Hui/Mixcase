The norman dynasty had a major political, cultural and military impact on medieval europe and the near east. The normans were famed for their martial spirit and eventually for their christian piety, becoming exponents of the catholic orthodoxy into which they assimilated. They adopted the gallo-romance language of the frankish land they settled, their dialect becoming known as norman, normaund or norman french, an important literary language.
