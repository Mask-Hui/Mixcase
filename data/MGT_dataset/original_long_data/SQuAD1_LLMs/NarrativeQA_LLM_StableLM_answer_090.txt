The national art library (also called word and image department) at the victoria and albert museum collection catalog used to be kept in different formats including printed exhibit catalogs, and card catalogs. A computer system called modes cataloging system was used from the 1980s to the 1990s, but those electronic files were not available to the library users. All of the archival material at the national art library is using encoded archival description (ead). The victoria and albert museum has a computer system but most of the items in the collection, unless those were newly accessioned into the collection, probably do not show up in the computer
