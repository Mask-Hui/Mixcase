The passage states that to be recognized as a local church lay servant, the individual must be recommended by their pastor and church council or charge conference, complete the basic course for lay servant and reapply annually. To be recognized as a certified lay servant, the individual must be recommended by their pastor and church council or charge conference, complete the basic course and one advanced lay servant course, be interviewed by the district or conference committee on lay speaking, and reapply annually. They must also complete at least one advanced course every three years.
