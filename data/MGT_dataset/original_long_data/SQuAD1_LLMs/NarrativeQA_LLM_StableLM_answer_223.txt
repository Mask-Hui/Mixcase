The answer is: in october 2015, hoesung lee was designated as the chair of the ipcc, following the election of the new ipcc bureau. Before this election, the ipcc was led by his vice-chair ismail el gizouli, who was designated acting chair after the resignation of rajendra k. Pachauri in february 2015. The previous chairs were rajendra k. Pachauri, elected in may 2002; robert watson in 1997; and bert bolin in 1988. The chair is assisted by an elected bureau including vice-chairs, working group co-chairs
