The normans were a group of germanic people who settled in the region of modern-day turkey and armenia in the 9th century. The normans were originally from the north, and their kingdom was centered in modern-day france. Later, they became a dominant force in the region and were able to expand their territory through military conquest. In the early 10th century, they joined with the turkish people of anatolia, who were also germanic, in their efforts to destroy the armenians' vassal-states.
