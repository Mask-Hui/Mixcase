The ipcc trust fund was established by the united nations environment programme (unep) and the world meteorological organization (wmo) in 1989, with the goal of providing financial assistance to the organization to carry out its functions and to promote the sustainable development of the climate system. The unep and wmo are responsible for the cost of the secretary and the depute secretary, respectively. The amount of annual cash contributions to the trust fund is made by the wmo, unep, and by ipcc members. The scale of payments is determined by the ipcc panel, which is also responsible for considering and adopting by consensus
