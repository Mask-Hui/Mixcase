The neighborhood of sunnyside is located within fresno county on the southeast side of fresno. It is bounded by clovis avenue to the west, kings canyon avenue to the south and fresno avenue to the north. Although much of the neighborhood is within the city of fresno, much of it is a "county island" within fresno county. The majority of the neighborhood was developed in the 1950s through the 1970s. It is the home of the sunnyside country club which maintains a golf course designed by william p. Bell.
