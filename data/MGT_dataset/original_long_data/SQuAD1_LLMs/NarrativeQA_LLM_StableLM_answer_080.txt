Tertiary education refers to higher education beyond the compulsory education system. It is typically pursued by individuals who have not been accepted into a primary or secondary school. The completion of tertiary education is often seen as an indication of a more mature and developed individual, capable of contributing to the community and society. Tertiary education is also often seen as a requirement for entering higher education, and is a form of postgraduate study in many countries. In australia, tertiary education is also sometimes seen as an opportunity for students to gain practical experience, gain exposure to a wide range of job opportunities and enhance their employability skills.
