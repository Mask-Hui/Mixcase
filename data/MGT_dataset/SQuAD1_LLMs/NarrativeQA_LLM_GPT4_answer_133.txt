The function of the TARDIS is to travel through time and space, allowing the Doctor (and other characters) to explore different eras and dimensions. The TARDIS is also a symbol of the Doctor's ability to control time and space, as well as his ability to travel through different dimensions. The TARDIS is a central element of the Doctor's character and his adventures, serving as a vehicle for the Doctor to explore and interact with different cultures, species, and eras.
