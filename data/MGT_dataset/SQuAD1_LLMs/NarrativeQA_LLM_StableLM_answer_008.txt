In Fresno, the average temperature during the summer months is around 83 °F (28.3 °C). The coldest month is December, with average temperatures around 46.5 °F (8.1 °C), and there are 14 nights with freezing lows. The warmest month is January, with an average temperature of 97% of the total possible sunlight hours. The temperature is usually around 32°C (0.8°F) during the summer months. The area is classified as a semi-arid climate (Köppen BSh) with mild, moist winters and hot and dry summers.
