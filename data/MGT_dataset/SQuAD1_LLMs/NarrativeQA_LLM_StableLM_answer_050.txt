The Aare (German for "Rhine") is a major river that flows generally westward and joins the Rhine River at the town of Constance, Switzerland. The Aare has a water discharge of over 1,000 m3/s (35,000 cu ft/s) and provides more than a fifth of the discharge at the Dutch border. The Aare also contains the waters from the 4,274 m (14,022 ft) summit of Finsteraarhorn, the highest point of the Rhine basin.
