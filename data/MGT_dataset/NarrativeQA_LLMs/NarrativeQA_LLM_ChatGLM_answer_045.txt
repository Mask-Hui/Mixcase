Why does Christopher accept blame for Will's crime?

 Christopher accepts blame for Will's crime because he realizes the enormity of his sin and the consequences that will follow. Despite his initial desire for revenge, he ultimately decides to take responsibility for his actions and face the punishment that will come his way. Additionally, he is willing to do whatever it takes to help Will escape and avoid further consequences for his actions. Through his actions, Christopher helps to ensure that Will's grandfather's possessions are returned to him, and he is able to live a life of freedom and happiness after his crime.
