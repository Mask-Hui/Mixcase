Bishop Drake gets shot by Anthony Cigliutti, a mafia boss who believes that Bishop had his sons killed, during a confrontation at the Stratosphere Hotel and Casino in Las Vegas. Despite Bishop's denial of having anything to do with the killing of Cigliutti's sons, Cigliutti shoots Bishop, leading to a gunfight that leaves several people, including bounty hunters Choco and Ed, severely wounded. The only survivor of the incident is Domino.
