Mr. Peters is Tom's nemesis throughout the novel. Mr. Peters was trying to buy Tom out of some of his inventions, but Tom refused. The animosity between the two only grew deeper. It appears that Mr. Peters was trying to scam Tom by threatening his friend Mr. Damon's financial stability. Also, Mr. Peters was trying to operate a motor boat without a license. All these shady occurrences set the mood and the plot of the novel, where one of Mr. Tom's airships was stolen and then Mr. Damon mysteriously disappeared.
