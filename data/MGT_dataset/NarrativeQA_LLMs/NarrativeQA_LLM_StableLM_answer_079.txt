The purpose of the fiddlefest concert is to raise money for the Opus 118 program, which has been cut due to budget cuts. The film's protagonist, Roberta Guaspari, was left out of a job and had to resort to finding a job as a substitute violin teacher to help her get back on her feet. The concert was organized to raise money for the program and was attended by famous musicians such as Mark O'Connor, Michael Tree, Charles Veal Jr., Arnold Steinhardt, Karen Briggs, Sandra Park, Diane Monroe, and Joshua Bell.
