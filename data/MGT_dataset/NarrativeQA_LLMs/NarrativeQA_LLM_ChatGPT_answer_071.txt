Diana puts a gun to her own head because she is in despair after Ahmed tells her that he is going to send her away. She pleads with him and confesses her own love, but he remains firm in his decision. In her desperation and heartbreak, Diana reaches for a revolver and attempts to end her life in the same way as her father. However, Ahmed manages to wrest the gun from her, causing the bullet to go astray, and then declares his love for her, finally bringing them together.
