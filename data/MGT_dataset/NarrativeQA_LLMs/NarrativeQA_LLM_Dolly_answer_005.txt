The question is asking about the events that occurred before a shoot-out began between the police and a mobsters at the Ambassador Hotel in Detroit. The answer to this question is: The vision of Elvis appears a second time and reassures Clarence that things are going well. Clarence manages to fake a story to a corrupt cop, Donowitz, that the drugs were given to him by a corrupt cop. The cop, Donowitz, agrees to the sale and the money is delivered to Clarence and Alabama in Mexico.
