Zurich, Switzerland, is a fictional location featured in the TV series "The Sopranos".
According to the show, Zurich is a large city in Switzerland that has become a world-wide hub for the human and crime organizations that control organized crime in New Jersey. 
One of the main characters in the show, Bobby Cutler, is offered a position as a Soprano in the fictional JamesInterval Security, a company based out of Zurich, Switzerland.
The company's headquarters are in a building which shares the same floor with the offices for TGI Fridays.
