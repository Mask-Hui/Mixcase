Diana is an independent and adventurous woman, who grew up with freedom not normally allowed to girls at that time. She is initially portrayed as disinterested in love and refuses a proposal of marriage. However, her experiences as a captive of the Sheik Ahmed Ben Hassan make her realize her love for him. She is described as self-loathing at times, but eventually gains Ahmed's trust and regains her own confidence. Overall, Diana is a complex and dynamic character.
