Ted decided to fight his fear of flying because he became traumatized during an unnamed war and had a pathological fear of flying. He is unable to hold a responsible job and is unable to communicate with Elaine Dickinson, who left him. He boards a Boeing 707 (Trans American Flight 209) from Los Angeles to Chicago, hoping to win her back, but she rebuffs him. After dinner is served, many of the passengers fall ill, and the cockpit crew, including pilot Clarence Oveur (Peter Graves) and co-pilot Roger Murdock (Kareem Abdul-Jabbar), have also been affected, leaving no one to fly the plane. Striker is convince to fly the plane by Steve McCroskey and Dr. Rumack. Despite the stress, Striker manages to land the plane safely with Kramer's advice.
