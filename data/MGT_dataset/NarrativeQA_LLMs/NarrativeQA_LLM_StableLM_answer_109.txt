Siddhartha decides to live out the rest of his life in the presence of the spiritually inspirational river. He resolves to carry on his quest alone and eventually returns to Kamala, the most beautiful woman he has ever seen. After his wife dies, he continues to live a humble life, seeking the teachings of the Buddha in the presence of the river. He eventually becomes a wealthy man and Kamala's lover, despite the fact that they are both physically present at the river, due to their shared spiritual journey.
