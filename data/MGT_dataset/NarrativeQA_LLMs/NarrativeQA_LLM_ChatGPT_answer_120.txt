Tulkinghorn is concerned about Lady Dedlock's secret that could potentially threaten the interests of Sir Leicester. He is worried that the secret could be revealed and is constantly watching Lady Dedlock and even enlists her maid to spy on her. Tulkinghorn is also concerned about any loose ends that could connect Nemo to the Dedlocks, so he enlists Inspector Bucket to run Jo out of town. Tulkinghorn is trying to protect the interests of Sir Leicester and maintain the status quo.
