The protagonist of this story is Virginia St. John, a renowned beauty who is newlywed and the newlywed wife of an English bishop. She is a celebrated intellectual and has a rational determination to convince her husband of the existence of Hell. The novel opens with her traveling abroad on a steamship Australasian, sailing from Melbourne to London. She is on her way to meet her new husband, but the ship's boiler explodes, and the ship sinks with the loss of almost all on board.
