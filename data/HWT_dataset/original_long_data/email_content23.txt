Note attached sdg&e press release calling on governor davis to use his emergency powers to remedy the accelerating energy crisis by taking a number of actions, e.g., directing local air districts to temporarily lift emissions limits for in-state power plants so that the plants which have met or exceeded their air-emissions limits can run. In addition, sdg&e reported it made an emergency filing with ferc requesting an immediate reinstatement of price caps on interstate natural gas pipeline transportation to the ca border.
